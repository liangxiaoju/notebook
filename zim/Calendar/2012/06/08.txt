====== 星期五 08  6月 2012 ======

TAGS: gdb, u-boot, qemu

在qemu中运行u-boot:
qemu-system-arm -nographic -M versatilepb -m 64 -kernel u-boot-spl -S -s

启动gdb:
arm-unknown-linux-gnueabi-gdb u-boot-spl

链接qemu:
target remote :1234

gdb常用命令:
	n				next
	c				continue
	b				breakpoint
	d				del breakpoint
	info registers
