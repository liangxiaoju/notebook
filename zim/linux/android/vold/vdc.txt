====== vdc ======
Created 星期二 15 十月 2013

dump:
vdc dump

volume:
vdc volume list
    list volumes
vdc volume mount <path>
    mount volume
vdc volume unmount <path> [force|force_and_revert]
    force -- force umount
    revert -- undo the crypto mapping
vdc volume share <path> <method>
vdc volume unshare <path> <method>
vdc volume shared <path> <method>
    method -- 'ums'

storage:
vdc storage users
    list storage user

asec:
vdc asec list
vdc asec create <container-id> <size_mb> <fstype> <key> <ownerUid> <isExternal>
vdc asec finalize <container-id>
vdc asec fixperms <container-id> <gid> <filename>
vdc asec destroy <container-id> [force]
vdc asec mount <namespace-id> <key> <owerUid>
vdc asec unmount <container-id> [force]
vdc asec rename <old_id> <new_id>
vdc asec path <container-id>
vdc asec fspath <container-id>

obb:
vdc obb list
vdc obb mount <filename> <key> <ownerGid>
vdc obb unmount <sourcefile> [force]
vdc obb path <sourcefile>

xwarp:
vdc xwarp enable
vdc xwarp disable
vdc xwarp status

cryptfs:
vdc cryptfs checkpw <passwd>
vdc cryptfs restart
vdc cryptfs cryptocomplete
vdc cryptfs enablecrypto <wipe|inplace> <passwd>
vdc cryptfs changepw <newpasswd>
vdc cryptfs verifypw <passwd>
