sdcard 挂载流程:

mount (system,sdcard_rw):
      sdcard --> /mnt/secure/staging

kill:
        杀掉所有打开"staging/autorun.inf"文件的进程

rename:
        /mnt/secure/staging/android_secure --> /mnt/secure/staging/.android_secure
        如果不存在就新建;如果android_secure不是文件夹将不能挂载.

bind (root):
     /mnt/secure/staging/.android_secure --> /mnt/secure/asec

mount (root):
      tmpfs --> /mnt/secure/staging/.android_secure
      掩盖.android_secure下的内容

move (system,sdcard_rw):
     /mnt/secure/staging --> /sdcard
