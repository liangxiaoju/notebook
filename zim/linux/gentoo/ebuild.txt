====== ebuild ======
Created 星期四 24 四月 2014

ebuild - a low level interface to the Portage system

* ebuild 分步编译
'''ebuild your-ebuild-file unpack/prepare/configure/compile/install/qmerge'''

* ebuild 打包
'''ebuild your-ebuild-file package'''
or
'''emerge xxx --buildpkg'''
and find *.tbz2 in /usr/portage/packages/

* ebuild 修改
修改完之后需要重新签名 '''ebuild your-ebuild-file digest'''

* 修改配置参数
通过ebuild的变量EXTRA_ECONF可以改变软件包./configure时的参数
'''EXTERA_ECONF="--enable-static" emerge xxx'''

* see also
