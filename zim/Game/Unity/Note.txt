====== Note ======
Created Friday 19 May 2017

* Coroutine
Coroutine will auto stop while gameobject disabled;

* Invoke
Invoke will not auto stop while gameobject disabled, it will stop on destroy or CancelInvoke;

* OnTriggerEnter2D
OnTriggerEnter2D(Collider2D other) may be called with other.gameObject disabled;

* StopCoroutine
StopCoroutine(name) will only stop 'name' coroutine, if you yield return 'othername' coroutine
in the 'name' coroutine, StopCoroutine(name) will not stop 'othername' coroutine;
